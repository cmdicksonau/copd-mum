My Hospital Stay Checklist

Ensure post-discharge safety and continuity safeguards. Mapped to Lung Foundation Australia (October 2025) targets.

  • Your GP needs to review your symptoms, medications, and clinical record within 7 days of discharge.
  • Confirm you have a physical or digital copy of your signed Action Plan easily accessible (e.g., on the fridge).
  • Verified by a pharmacist, nurse, or doctor before leaving the hospital to ensure maximum medication delivery.
  • Check if you are referred to a Pulmonary Rehab program or physiotherapy-guided exercise regimen to support lung strength.
  • Confirm with your nurse/GP that your vaccines are up to date: Influenza, Pneumococcal, and SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19).
  • For patients with advanced stages of COPD, ensure discussions concerning palliative care and advance care directives are logged.
